Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026  ·  Effective immediately upon registration

1. Introduction

Nidaa is a humanitarian assistance platform that connects people in need with verified volunteers, psychologists, and organizations. We are committed to protecting your personal data and being transparent about how we use it.

By registering or using Nidaa, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

When you register or use Nidaa, we collect:

  • Personal identifiers: Full name, email address, phone number
  • Account data: Role (Beneficiary, Volunteer, Psychologist, Organization), password (encrypted)
  • Location data: Address or coordinates you provide when submitting help requests
  • Support data: Details of help requests, psychological support requests, and communications on the platform
  • Usage data: Login timestamps, activity logs for security and improvement purposes

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To match beneficiaries with volunteers and psychologists
  • To verify the identity of professionals (psychologists, organizations)
  • To send notifications about request status updates
  • To contact applicants regarding account approval
  • To improve the platform and ensure its security
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Psychological Support Confidentiality

All psychological support sessions and requests are treated with the highest level of confidentiality. Psychologists on the platform are bound by professional ethics. Anonymous requests are fully anonymized — we do not link them to your personal account in any visible way.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited information:

  • With volunteers assigned to your help request (name and contact for coordination)
  • With verified organizations for coordination purposes
  • With platform administrators for approval and moderation
  • With legal authorities if required by law

6. Data Security

Your password is stored using bcrypt encryption — we cannot read it. All API communications use JWT authentication. We implement security measures to protect against unauthorized access.

7. Your Rights

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data is removed within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.
